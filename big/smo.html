Social Media Optimization (SMO)

SMO (Social Media Optimization) Is the process of integrate Social Media networks into your website to grow the organization message and presence online as a strategy to increase awareness of your products and services, connect with new customers and current customers and enhance the image of the company.

“Social Media Optimization is essentially using social media as a catalyst to grow your company’s online presence. Where some companies tend to just set up a profile on Instagram, Facebook or Twitter to be where their customers are, SMO is about strategically creating, building and maximizing your social media plan to connect with your target audience.”1

Today, social media plays a big role on marketing strategies, “Studies show that companies that do not succeed with social media optimization are neglecting social media optimization, which offers the benefits of strengthening a brand, lead generation, increased visibility in the online space, and better connections with a company's audience.”2 This means that social media is an updated way to approach and engage with customers and it is also an easy way to find you and connect with you.

SMO allows to make your brand stronger, generate customers, get online visibility and connect with customers. One of the tools to accomplish your goals is by the use of Keywords research. A Keyword research is the identification of phrases and main words to target an audience so you can create relevant content for them. This allows you to connect searchers to your site, so the users can find it easy to find your website.
